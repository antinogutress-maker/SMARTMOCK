Combined Test Procedure

  1. Take ~3kg (Std) or ~5kg (Mod) air-dried soil passing a 4.75mm sieve. Add a specific initial % of water and mix.
  2. Place the mould on a solid base. Place the guide sleeve directly on the soil surface.
  3. Fill and compact the soil in 3 layers (Standard) or 5 layers (Modified).
  4. For every layer, lift the internal rammer exactly to its limit (310mm or 450mm) and drop it, ensuring the foot makes direct contact with the soil. Apply 25 uniformly distributed blows per layer.
  5. Remove the collar, strike off excess soil flush with the 127.3mm high mould.
  6. Weigh the compacted soil to find Bulk Density. Take a sample to dry in an oven to find Moisture Content.
  7. Plot Dry Density vs Moisture Content to locate the peak of the curve (MDD and OMC).
