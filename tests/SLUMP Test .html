Standard Notes for Loksewa

Objective: To determine the workability and consistency of fresh concrete.

Apparatus Specifications (Crucial for MCQs):

  • Slump Cone: Top Dia = 10 cm, Bottom Dia = 20 cm, Height = 30 cm. Made of metal (min 1.6mm thick).
  • Tamping Rod: 16 mm diameter, 60 cm length, bullet-pointed at one end.

Procedure (IS 1199):

  1. Place the cleaned, oiled mould on a smooth, horizontal, non-porous base plate.
  2. Fill the mould with concrete in 4 approximately equal layers (by height/depth).
  3. Tamp each layer exactly 25 times uniformly with the bullet end of the tamping rod.
  4. Strike off the excess concrete flush with the top using a trowel or the rod.
  5. Remove the cone immediately and slowly in a vertical direction.
  6. Measure the subsidence (slump) in millimeters from the top of the cone to the highest point of the specimen.
